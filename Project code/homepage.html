Facilities Management Office - HKMU

If you have lost something valuable or items with personal data at HKMU (e.g., USB, wallet, Octopus card, mobile phone, and computer), please click "Lost Item Registration" to report your lost or complete the Report Form and submit it to the security control rooms below.

Note

It is the University's policy to keep lost items for one month. Those remaining unclaimed thereafter will be disposed of in suitable ways. Claim of sundry supplies items (e.g., umbrella, water bottle, stationery, and glasses) will be at the self-service corners near the security control rooms on MC, JCC, and IOH. Perishable items such as food and drinks will be disposed of immediately or kept for a shorter period of time according to their condition.
